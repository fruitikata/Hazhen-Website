Rules & Regulations


Return and Exchange Policy:


•Buyers can request a refund within three days of receiving a product if there are quality issues, supported by an unboxing video. The seller will cover return shipping costs.
•To process a refund, contact the seller and send the product to the provided address within the three-day window. No returns will be accepted after this period, and only one return request is allowed per order.
•For orders that have been placed but not yet shipped, cancellation must be made within one hour. After that, refunds are not possible.

Food Delivery Service Terms:


•For food purchasing services, once payment is made, the delivery will be sent to the address and phone number provided by the buyer.
•Payments are final and non-refundable. Be sure to double-check your address, name, and phone number before completing the order.
•We do not accept rude and impatient clients

If an order is shipped or delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer, the responsibility lies with the customer. Please double-check all information before submitting it.
